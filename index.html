About DIAPS

DIAPS (Disaster Information Aggregation and Prediction Software) helps you assess potential natural disaster risks for any location worldwide. The system combines weather forecasts, historical earthquake data, and geographical information to provide risk assessments.

Weather Forecasts

Get 3-day weather forecasts for any location with temperature, precipitation, and wind data.

Earthquake Monitoring

View recent earthquake activity within 100km of your selected location.

Risk Assessment

Receive risk evaluations for floods, landslides, heavy rain, and tsunamis based on location data.
